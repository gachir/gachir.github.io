About

Our club was formed in 1977 by a group of enthusiastic founders. Today, we have over 100 members and we are now located in a large facility offering extensive indoor and outdoor spaces. We love new members and welcome anyone wishing to join a vibrant club and community. If you love trains or want to learn more, contact us today. We also have multiple open days throughout the year for the general public.
