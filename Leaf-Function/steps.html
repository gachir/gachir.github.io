Taking atmospheric and leaf measurements

You will need:
~Infrared thermomenter
~Pocket weather meter

Step 1

Make sure measurement leaves are in full sun and facing the direction of the sun

Step 2:

Turn on pocket weather meter and measure air temperature (Celsius) and relaitive humidity

Gently move the pocket weather meter back and forth (1cm movements)

Be sure to wait at 30 seconds before recording and record

Step 3:

Immediately measure leaf surface temperature with infrared thermometer
